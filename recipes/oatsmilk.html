Oatmilks


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all purpose flour (or whole wheat)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 5 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk or water
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

  3. In a large mixing bowl combine the dry ingredients and mix well until combined.
  4. Add in the milk mixture alternating with half of the hot milk for about an hour.

  6. Stir in remaining milk if needed then knead into dough shape using your hands.
  7. Place on floured surface and turn out onto lightly floured work surface.
  8. Bake at 375 degrees F for approximately one hour.
